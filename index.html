Wenhe Liu

Greetings! I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Language Technologies Institute (LTI) at Carnegie Mellon University, working with Professor Alexander G. Hauptmann. I received my Ph.D. degree in Centre for Artificial Intelligence & Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney. My supervisor is Professor Yi Yang and my co-supervisor is Dr. Xiaojun Chang .

My research interest is mainly on machine learning and its applications and computer vision.
